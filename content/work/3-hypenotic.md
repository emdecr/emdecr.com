A founding Canadian B Corp working with social enterprises, non-profits, and mission-driven organizations.

Over four years I grew from WordPress theming into leading development across the team — introducing Vue.js and Nuxt.js, building headless CMS architectures, mentoring junior developers, and owning infrastructure and deployment.

**Key work:**

- Built custom WordPress themes and Vue.js/Nuxt.js applications for client projects across social enterprise, non-profit, and advocacy sectors
- Advocated for and introduced server-side rendering (Nuxt.js) to improve SEO and performance for client sites
- Led technical scoping, documentation, and budgeting for client RFPs
- Mentored interns and junior developers — designed informal onboarding curriculum that balanced learning with delivery
- Managed LEMP/LAMP servers on Linode, handling deployment, security, and performance

**What this chapter taught me:** How to grow systems and people at the same time — and how to care deeply about the work without holding too tightly to it.
