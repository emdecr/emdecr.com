Open-source platform for secure collaboration in technical and regulated environments.

I joined as a web developer and grew into the sole technical owner of the entire marketing web surface — responsible for front-end architecture, a large WordPress codebase, cross-functional coordination with Marketing, Product, Design, and Sales, and the GTM tooling pipeline that connected them.

**Key work:**

- Led a full site redesign and rebrand across hundreds of pages, managing two external developers and shipping on a tight, non-negotiable timeline
- Built a reusable component library (60+ components) with ACF, enabling non-developers to update content independently
- Partnered with Product to launch a self-serve trial flow, overhauling CTAs across all primary marketing entry points
- Designed and shipped an inbound-led outbound pipeline from scratch — integrating Clearbit, Clay, and Outreach for lead identification, enrichment, and routing
- Integrated and maintained Marketo, Salesforce, GA4, Mutiny, ZoomInfo, and other GTM tools end to end

**What this chapter taught me:** A marketing site isn't a static artifact — it's a living interface between product, mission, trust, and revenue. This role expanded my definition of what "web developer" means.
